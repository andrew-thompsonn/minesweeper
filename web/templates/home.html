"Not as Boring as the Original"

Classic Singleplayer

Play classic minesweeper on standard difficulties - Easy (10 x 10 and 10 mines), Medium (16 x 16 and 40 mines), and hard (16 x 30 and 99 mines). In Minesweeper Improved, it is impossible to click a mine on the first square, and at a minimum nine squares will be revealed on the first click.

Play Against the Computer

Minesweeper improved contains the classic minesweeper we all know and love, but with a twist. Multiplayer options allow for the player to compete side by side with the computer, with an array of difficulties and skill levels. The computer is designed to be competitive, and is capable of making mistakes when guessing.

Watch the Computer Dominate

The game contains a mode for watching the computer clear mines as fast as possible. In this mode, the computer is less likely to make mistakes. When move is encountered where a guess must be made, it will calcualte the probability of mines existing in each square. Then execute the move with the lowest probability of a loss.
